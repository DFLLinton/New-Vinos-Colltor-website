It is the family home of Mark Lavery and Arbell Lowther.

Our main activity is cultivating vines and making wine.

We use the most natural, environmentally friendly processes available.

Colltor is a traditional Catalan, stone Masia, which dates back to 1710. The house was abandoned and fell into ruin but we have restored it carefully over the years.

The Farmhouse

The stone farmhouse dates back to 1710. The farmhouse incorporates a bodega, where we produce the wine.

The Vineyard

We began producing wine in 2014 and joined the DO Pla de Bages in 2016.

The Yurt

A handcrafted traditional Mongolian Yurt, situated in the woods with views of the old village and Montserrat in the distance.
