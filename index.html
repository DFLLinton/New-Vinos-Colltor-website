Colltor is a 500m2 farmhouse situated 1km outside of Santa Maria dâ€™Olo

Vinos Colltor's main activity is cultivating our vines, producing and comercializing the wine from them. The whole process is done in the most natural way possible.

We also look after the almonds, olive trees, oak trees and the original stone walls.

All with the idea of recovering the habitat as it was before. You can wander through the 11 hectares of vines and woodland and see the only vineyard in Catalunya with views of Montserrat and the Pyrennees at the same time.

The Farmhouse

The farmhouse dates back to 1710 and offers 450m2 living space. At the moment, there is a double room, a twin room and a single room available.

The Vineyard

2020 is our 6th year of wine production and we are proud to introduce our two wines as part of the D.O. Pla de Bages for the first time.

The Yurt

The yurt is handcrafted from sustainable sources. It is 6 metres in diameter with a canvas exterior, felt exterior lining and cotton inner lining.
