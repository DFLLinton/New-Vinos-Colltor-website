Tourist Destination

Santa Maria d'Olo is situated in the Moianes, a natural region within Catalunya, which is made up of 10 municipalities.

Activities

There are a multitude of places to visit in the area surronding our home. To name a few, we are located close to Montserrat, Rupit and Vic's famous market.

We are also located next to a variety of interesting and unique activities and possible outings. For example, one could play golf, rock climb or go horse riding.

We have the good fortune to be only 20 minutes by car from 3 One Star Michelin restaurants, and 2 others of a similar category. There are many other good places with more economical prices. To name a few, Can Jubany, Fonda Sala and Mon San Benet.
