The Yurt

It is 6 metres in diameter with a canvas exterior, felt exterior and cotton inner lining.

Facilities

There are two traditional Japanese futon sofas, which can be easily converted into fantastically comfortable double beds. If the weather is against you, there is an indoor dining room suite and a wood burning stove to keep you warm on even the coldest night. Double doors open out onto your extensive private outdoor area, where you will find a wooden picnic table.

The site has a covered outside kitchen area,supplied with a full range of equipment including a top-of-the-line gas barbecue. There is a traditional stone sink for washing. The exclusive bathhouse is furnished with a stone sink, shower and composting WC. All the hot water and electricity is generated by solar panels.

There is plenty of space for extra tents (by arrangement) and parking. A starter package is available by arrangement. All bedding, towels, firewood and equipment are provided as part of the package.
