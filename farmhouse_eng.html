The Farmhouse

The farmhouse dates back to 1710 and offers 450m2 living space.

Facilities

Of the 7 bedrooms, we have prepared 3 for our guests in this new stage in which offers rooms to let as rural tourism. Bed and breakfast with options for pre booked dinners. There is one double bedroom, one twin and one single. There is also room to add supplementary beds.

We have also restored the dining room and living room area all with antique furniture . We have tried to leave everything as it was. Being then perfect.

We have the capacity for seven people across the three rooms. We also offer the oppurtunity for pre-booked dinners.

Our acommodation offers: internet access, a swimming pool, breakfast, a television, a barbecue and a space to park cars.
