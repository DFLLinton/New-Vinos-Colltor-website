The Farmhouse

The farmhouse dates back to 1710 and offers 450m2 living space.

Facilities

We have converted the entire top floor of the farmhouse into an attractive and spacious, self-contained apartment, available as a single exclusive booking.

The accommodation comprises 3 bedrooms; a double room with an antique four-poster bed, a twin room and a single room (there is an option for a further double sofa bed).

There is a large light and airy central living and dining area. A wood burning stove keeps the whole apartment toasty warm in winter.

There is a simple fully equipped kitchenette for the self catering.

There is of course a large bathroom. All sheets and towels are provided.

Our guests can enjoy exclusive use of the beautiful swimming pool, terrace and barbeque (charcoal is NOT provided).

TV, full internet access and parking available.

Dogs welcome.

Breakfast if required is additional and bookable in advance.

It is possible to reserve a dinner or lunch by special arrangement. Price on request.
