Tourist Destination

Santa Maria D’Olo is situated in the centre of Catalunya, 50 minutes from Barcelona, 50 minutes from the Costa Brava and 50 minutes from the Pyrenees.

Activities

The are a multitude of places to visit and things to do in the surrounding area. We are located close to Montserrat, Rupit and Vic’s famous market.

Local activities available include golf, horse riding, rock climbing and hiking to suggest a few.

There are many exciting and challenging cycle routes both road and off-road. Or follow any number of well sign posted walking trails through the woods and forests.

We have the good fortune to be very close to a number of excellent restaurants dotted around the area, including 3 with Michelin Stars.
