The Vineyard

Colltor shiraz is our top wine, a monovarietal with intense colour and fine aromas.

Enotourism

Colltor merlot coupage is our medium range wine. Aged in American oak casks the merlot gives the wine body and structure while shiraz and cabernet sauvignon add subtleties: a really good honest wine.

One can visit the bodega and vineyard. Taste our two wines accompanied by a tapa in beautiful surroundings overlooking Montserrat: 12 euros per person, approx 40 min

2020 was our 6th year of wine production and we were proud to introduce our two wines as part of the D.O. Pla de Bages for the first time.

From January to May, we prune the vine. From May to June, we prepare the vines and prune the green. From July to August, our work consists of spraying and maintenance. By the end of September, we have harvested the grapes and October to February consists of celler operations.
